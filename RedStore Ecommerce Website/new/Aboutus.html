About the Project

Our project is an E-commerce website that aims to provide a user-friendly platform for online shopping. With a wide range of products and secure payment options, we strive to enhance the online shopping experience for our customers. The website offers various features such as product categorization, customer reviews, and personalized recommendations to make the shopping process efficient and enjoyable.
